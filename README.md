# Ride Optimization & Customer Retention Analytics  

## 📌 Objective  
Reduce ride cancellations and customer churn through data-driven allocation and incentives.  

## ⚡ Challenges  
- High cancellations during peak demand.  
- Increasing customer churn due to poor service availability.  

## 🛠️ Actions  
- Extracted and cleaned large-scale ride and driver datasets using **SQL** for accurate trend analysis.  
- Built **Excel dashboards** to monitor ride demand, cancellations, and driver utilization KPIs in near real time.  
- Applied **Python** to analyze customer behavior and identify churn triggers.  
- Designed **zone allocation models** to balance ride supply across peak-demand zones and reduce cancellations.  
- Provided marketing with **data-backed insights** to design customer incentive programs.  
- Conducted regular reviews with operations and management to validate and refine strategies.  

## ✅ Outcomes  
- Reduced cancellations by **13%**.  
- Achieved a **5.3% decrease in customer churn**.  

## 🧑‍💻 Skills Used  
`SQL` `Python` `Excel` `Retention Analytics`
